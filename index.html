FAQ

Do I need to register a business to earn online?

Not always. If you're freelancing as a sole proprietor, you can operate under your own name. For higher incomes or selling products, registering a business may be beneficial. Learn more here.

How do I pay taxes on freelance income?

All income must be reported to the CRA. You may need to register for a GST/HST number if your revenue exceeds the threshold. Visit the CRA site.

Can Work Permit or PR holders earn online legally?

Yes, if the activity aligns with permit conditions. Always consult immigration guidelines. Check IRCC rules.

Are platforms like Upwork and Fiverr legal in Canada?

Yes, as long as income is declared and legal terms are respected.
